## Tier 1: Most Comprehensive European Salary Data

### 1. **Glassdoor** (glassdoor.com, glassdoor.de, glassdoor.ie, etc.)

- **Coverage**: All major European countries
- **Strength**: Company-specific salaries with employee-submitted data
- **Cybersecurity positions**: SOC Analyst, Security Engineer, CISO, Penetration Tester
- **Filters**: Location, experience level, company size
- **Example**: Search "SOC Analyst Munich" shows salary range + company reviews
- **Limitation**: Self-reported (can be inflated/deflated)

### 2. **Levels.fyi** (levels.fyi)

- **Coverage**: Major tech hubs (Berlin, Dublin, Amsterdam, Zurich, London)
- **Strength**: Tech company compensation (base + equity + bonus breakdown)
- **Cybersecurity positions**: Security Engineer levels (L3-L7)
- **Best for**: Big tech companies (Google, Meta, Amazon, Microsoft)
- **Data quality**: Verified through offer letters
- **Limitation**: Limited coverage of non-tech companies, MSSPs, consulting

### 3. **PayScale** (payscale.com)

- **Coverage**: 15+ European countries
- **Strength**: Cost of living adjusted salaries, skill-based pay data
- **Cybersecurity positions**: All major roles with skill breakdowns
- **Unique feature**: "Am I paid fairly?" calculator
- **Example**: Shows how Python skills vs. CISSP certification affects salary
- **Limitation**: Requires survey participation for full data access

### 4. **SalaryExpert** (salaryexpert.com)

- **Coverage**: 190+ countries including all EU nations
- **Strength**: Cost of living comparisons, tax calculations
- **Cybersecurity positions**: SOC Analyst, Security Consultant, CISO
- **Unique feature**: Shows net salary after taxes by country
- **Example**: "SOC Analyst Germany" shows €54,424 gross = €XX,XXX net
- **Best for**: Cross-country comparisons (e.g., Dublin vs. Amsterdam)

## Tier 2: European-Specific Platforms

### 5. **EuroSalaries.com** (eurosalaries.com)

- **Coverage**: EU27 + UK + Switzerland + Norway
- **Strength**: IT sector focus, quarterly updates
- **Cybersecurity positions**: Detailed role breakdowns
- **Best for**: Comparing salaries across multiple EU countries side-by-side

### 6. **TechPays.eu** (techpays.eu)

- **Coverage**: Western Europe (Germany, Netherlands, UK, France, Ireland)
- **Strength**: Tech-specific salary data, startup vs. corporate breakdowns
- **Cybersecurity positions**: Security Engineer, DevSecOps, SOC Analyst

### 7. **Honeypot** (honeypot.io/pages/how-much-do-developers-earn)

- **Coverage**: Germany, Netherlands, Austria, Switzerland
- **Strength**: Developer-focused but includes DevSecOps, Security Engineer

- **Best for**: German market (very accurate Berlin/Munich data)

## Pro Tips for Salary Research

1. **Use multiple sources**: Cross-reference 3+ platforms for accuracy
2. **Filter by company size**: Enterprise vs. startup salaries vary significantly
3. **Check posting dates**: Cybersecurity salaries rising 8-12% annually (2023-2024)
4. **Include equity/bonus**: Total compensation often 15-30% higher than base
5. **Factor cost of living**: €60K Dublin ≠ €60K Munich (rent, taxes differ)
6. **Consider remote options**: Some companies pay location-adjusted, others don't
7. **Certification premium**: CISSP adds ~€8-12K, OSCP ~€10-15K in Europe
